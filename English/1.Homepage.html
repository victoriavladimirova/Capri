Capri Hotel is situated in a quiet and calm area at the very coast between Ravda and Nesebar. It is a preferred destination for relax and tourism. The pleasant atmosphere and comfort are preconditions for an unforgettable and peaceful holiday for you, your family and friends.

Welcome!

The Hotel has 16 double rooms /all with option for an extra bed/ and 2 apartments. All of them are modern and stylishly furnished as they impress with their coziness and comfort, each apartment has toilet, LCD TV, WI-FI, air-conditioner, minibar, hairdryer and a terrace.
